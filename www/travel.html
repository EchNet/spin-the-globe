TRAVEL THE GLOBE

This application demonstrates the ability of KAYAK GLOBE to integrate with any HTML page, using Javascript and DHTML (Ajax optional). Through Javascript, links and buttons on the page may be used to control the globe, and the contents of the page may be updated to reflect the state of the globe.

To run this application, click the GO button below. (The button is just a plain old HTML input control.) The globe spins to a location chosen at random by the javascript on this page. (The location could as easily be chosen by the server.) Once the globe stops spinning, the location is displayed (again in plain old HTML) underneath the globe.
