KAYAK GLOBE

KAYAK GLOBE is a prototype web component developed by James Echmalian for kayak.com.

What does it do?

The component displays and animates a globe. The globe can turn on its axis. Its attitude and tilt may be adjusted, as can its apparent distance from the viewer.

How is it implemented?

The Flash component is implemented in ActionScript 3.0.

Various libraries, tools and utilities play a part in the implementation:

  • The PaperVision 3D graphics library is used to render the bitmapped sphere.
  • Some components make use of the Adobe Flex development tools and component library.
  • The SWFObject utility handles Flash Player version detection and Flash object embedding.

    • Any special requirements?

    Because of its reliance on ActionScript 3.0, the globe requires Flash Player 9.0 or beyond to run.

    You must enable Javascript to access some features.
